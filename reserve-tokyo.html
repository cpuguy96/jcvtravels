Tokyo, Japan

Behind the technological innovations that spring from its urban setting lies a country of tremendous heritage and culture. Whether you’re interested in soaking in the traditional bathhouses in the countryside or dancing in the buzzing clubs in the heart of Tokyo, you will find everything you’re looking for and then some. Witness the transfusion of the old with the new!

Tokyo has everything. A juggernaut of technology as well as a relic of tradition, Tokyo balances contrasting ideals. Known as the fashion capital of the east, the artistic influences are distinguishable all around you. Explore all that Tokyo has to offer and then some.
