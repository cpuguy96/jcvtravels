Montreux, Switzerland

Taking in your first breath of Swiss air is breathtaking. The Alpine region of Switzerland hosts an untapped reserve of nature’s bumps and curves. The exhilarating mountain ranges are ideal for outdoor activities on the snow. Warm yourself by indulging on a hot cup of coca in one of many lodges. What’re you waiting for? The slopes are calling you!

Nowhere else in the world will you be surrounded by the freashest air. The snow around you invites adventure. There are plenty of activities to keep you busy and to remind you of the fun that can be had in the cold. While this destination is on the chilly side, it makes the warmth much more pleasurabe.
