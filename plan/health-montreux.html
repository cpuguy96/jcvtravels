Health & Safety Information

Switzerland is relatively rule-oriented, and street crime is fairly uncommon. You should still watch your belongings at airports and train stations. Also keep your belongings safe while in city crowds to prevent being pickpocketed.

The weather in Montreux can be extremely changeable at any time of the year. Skiers and hikers should always be prepared for very cold and wet weather.

Hypothermia occurs when the body loses heat at a greater rate than it can produce it. A combination of wind, wet clothing, fatigue, and hunger can cause hypothermia even when the temperature is above freezing. Bring warm clothing and dress appropriately whenever you go outside.

There is a danger of avalanches at certain times of the year. Take care and observe all written notices and warnings.

With any trip, make sure to take out travel and medical insurance before you travel and have vaccinations up to date.

Required Vaccinations

  • Measles-Mumps-Rubella (MMR)
  • Diphtheria-Tetanus-Pertussis Vaccine
  • Varicella (Chickenpox)
  • Polio
  • Annual Flu Shot
