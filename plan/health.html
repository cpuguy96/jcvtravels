Health & Safety Information

For the most part, Baros is a safe destination to travel. The most likely danger is sunburn so make sure to use proper protection while outside.

Since you will be on island, water is also a big concern while vacationing. Make sure to take precaution while diving or swimming. Beware of possible strong currents and do not touch coral, shells or fish.

Crimes of violence are unusual, but there are cases of burglaries and theft. Make sure to keep all precious belongings locked in a safe while traveling.

With any trip, make sure to take out travel and medical insurance before you travel and have vaccinations up to date.

Required Vaccinations

  • Measles-Mumps-Rubella (MMR)
  • Diphtheria-Tetanus-Pertussis Vaccine
  • Varicella (Chickenpox)
  • Polio
  • Annual Flu Shot
